TAKE ACTION.

Many social reform movements concerning Criminal Justice have risen in our nation. Here is more information on them:

Black Lives Matter

The Black Lives Matter Movement is a grassroots movement that has quickly gained traction in our society, and is famous for protesting against issues regarding to African Americans, particularly the matters of criminal injustice, police brutality, and racial inequalities. The movement calls for ALL black lives, advocates for ALL black lives, and protests for ALL black lives. This movement has taken off on social media and across the nation. Learn more information on the #BLM movement on their website: https://blacklivesmatter.com/

American Civil Liberties Union:

The American Civil Liberties Union has been advocating for the civil rights of all for nearly 100 years.

Amnesty USA:

The AmnestyUSA Project advocates for human rights. Essentially, they try to help people who have been wrongfully imprisoned. They help refugees, migrants, women, children, the LGBTQ community, and extend their help to many more. To join their movement and take action, be sure to visit their website: https://www.amnestyusa.org/

How to help in your own community:

  • Call your state representative and voice your opinion.
  • Read the facts and educate yourself on the issue.
  • Take part in marches and share your thoughts on social media.
  • Join the movements listed above.
